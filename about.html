About

If you're a content professional, you've seen how AI has become good enough to do a version of what you do. And it's only getting better.

What's still valuable about the human part?

The essays on this site are my attempt to answer that question. Content strategy, storytelling, building, and what happens to creative work when AI changes the economics.

G'day, I'm Michael Ofei. 10+ years in editorial, most recently as managing editor at Backlinko, teaching marketing to marketers. I study why some ideas stick and others don't. I also build software with Claude Code — it's one of the most fun things I've done in years.

I don't have a publishing schedule, but you can sign up for my newsletter to read new essays when they're ready.
